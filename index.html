Courses

"No one size fits all following fix learning materials."

    How it works:

  1. You express interest using a «I am interested...» link (email)
  2. We get back to you when minimum number of participants is reached
  3. We agree on dates of meetings
  4. You pay (previous to meetings or cash on occasion)
  5. We enjoy our meetings ;-)

Beginner Courses

Beginner courses do not expect that you to have any knowledge in the area of programming.

The courses provide the essential knowledge and try to convey a fundamental understanding.

Course language is Swedish or English depending on the preference of the participants.

my first programming language

A "learning by doing" introduction course where you learn to think like a programmer by developing your own (simple) programming language.

You'll learn "the real thing", no toying around with shiny tools for "kids" that mean well but mostly convey a wrong idea.

The course enables you to learn other languages all by yourself as you now understand what programming is about.

Bring your own device, preferably Mac or Linux.
